Most maintenance teams underutilize their CMMS, using only 30-50% of available features. The article outlines a three-layer ROI framework progressing from reactive work order management through preventive maintenance to advanced reporting, arguing that teams must optimize processes and strategically activate high-leverage features to turn the software from an administrative expense into a profit driver.
