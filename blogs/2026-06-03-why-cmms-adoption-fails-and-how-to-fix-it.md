Low CMMS adoption stems from workflow friction rather than software deficiency, as organizations often mistake usage problems for training gaps when the real issue is that digital processes have become more cumbersome than traditional methods like whiteboards and paper notes. The article explains how maintenance teams can diagnose and fix adoption failures by reducing friction in day-to-day CMMS workflows.
